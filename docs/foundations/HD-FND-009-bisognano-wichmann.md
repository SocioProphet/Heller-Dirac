## Statement surface

In the vacuum representation of a Wightman / Haag-Kastler quantum field theory on Minkowski space, the modular flow of the wedge algebra with respect to the vacuum vector coincides with the Lorentz boost flow preserving that wedge.

For the right wedge `W`, with local algebra `A(W)` and vacuum vector `Omega`, Tomita-Takesaki theory gives a modular flow. Bisognano-Wichmann identifies that flow with the corresponding one-parameter boost group, up to the conventional `2 pi` normalization.

## Reading

This is the controlled place where modular time is physically interpretable: vacuum sector, wedge algebra, flat spacetime QFT. It is the reference theorem constraining Heller-Dirac time-theory claims.

## Scope

Applies to wedge algebras in the vacuum representation of suitable relativistic QFTs. It does not automatically apply to curved spacetime, non-vacuum sectors, arbitrary regions, or non-QFT systems.
