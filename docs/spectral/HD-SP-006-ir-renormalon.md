## Scope

This identifier records the Borel-plane singularity vocabulary used in renormalon analysis of large-order perturbative expansions.

## Borel-plane role

`HD-SP-006` is downstream of `HD-SP-004`. It concerns singularities and contour ambiguities that appear after passing from a divergent perturbative series to its Borel transform.

In gauge-theory language, IR-renormalon and UV-renormalon terminology separates positive-axis and negative-axis obstruction patterns in conventional Borel-plane coordinates. The exact normalization and singularity locations are scheme- and theory-dependent and are not fixed by this framework file.

## Ambiguity matching

Renormalon analysis often compares Borel-Laplace ambiguities with non-perturbative power-correction vocabulary, such as operator-product-expansion condensate terms. Heller-Dirac records the comparison surface only.
