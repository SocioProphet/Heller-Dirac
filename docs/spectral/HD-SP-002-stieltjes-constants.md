## Scope

This identifier records Stieltjes-constant vocabulary for zeta functions and spectral-zeta analogues.

The Riemann zeta baseline is the Laurent expansion at `s = 1`, where the constant term is `gamma_0 = gamma_E`.

## Spectral-zeta use

For a spectral zeta function with meromorphic continuation, regular-part coefficients at a pole may be treated as generalized Stieltjes-type data. Their existence and significance depend on the verified analytic structure of the specific zeta function.
