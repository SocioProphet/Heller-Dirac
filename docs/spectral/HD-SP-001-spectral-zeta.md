## Scope

For spectral data `(A, H, D)`, this identifier records the zeta-function vocabulary attached to traces of powers of `|D|` under appropriate summability and regularity hypotheses.

## Heat interface

The heat-kernel and Mellin-transform relation is recorded here as vocabulary and is developed further in `HD-SP-003`.

## Provenance

## Boundary
